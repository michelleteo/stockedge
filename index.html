Strategy primer

📞 Covered Calls

Sell call options on stocks you own to collect premium income every month.

  • Own 100+ shares of the stock
  • Sell a call 2–5% above current price
  • Target 30–45 day expiry, 0.20–0.40 delta
  • Keep the premium whether exercised or not

🏦 Warren Buffett

Buy wonderful companies at fair prices and hold them forever.

  • P/E below 25, ROE above 15%
  • Consistent earnings growth 5+ years
  • Low debt, strong free cash flow
  • Businesses you can understand

📈 Nicholas Darvas

Buy stocks breaking out of tight "boxes" on high volume and ride the trend.

  • Price forms a tight range (the "box")
  • Breakout on 2x+ average volume
  • Stop-loss just below the box floor
  • Only buy in strong uptrends
